About Sno-King Junior Golf

Coach's Philosophy

Coach Gerry offers free golf tips of coaching/instruction by providing you a wide range of information, on the course coaching, video analysis, training aids and reading materials.

My purpose is to coach junior golfers play at the higher level of golf competition for High School Team, Washington Junior Golf, and Junior Tours. By focusing on individual's natural ability on solid basic golf fundamentals of athletic posture, swing mechanic, focus and pre-shot routine.
